Rice

Description

you boil rice, but it's edible

ingredients

  • rice
  • water

steps

  1. wash the rice
    1. mix water with rice
    2. remove the water
    3. repeat until the removed water is clear instead of foggy
  2. place rice + equal part water (1:1) in an unheated pot, and cover
  3. turn up the heat until it boils
  4. set heat to low ,wait 15 minutes
  5. turn heat off, wait 10 minutes
  6. (optional) slap it inside of a sandwich, for better texture and some extra flavor.
