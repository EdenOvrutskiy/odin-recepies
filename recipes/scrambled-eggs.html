Scrambled Eggs

Description

you scramble eggs

ingredients

  • eggs
  • butter
  • bread(optional)

steps

  1. break eggs into a container
  2. mix until everything is yellow
  3. place butter onto a heated pan
  4. once butter is melted, pour the eggs on top
  5. continuously stir while they cook, otherwise they'll stick to the pan
  6. once they're a fluffy solid, place on a plate (don't wait too long or it becomes too dry)
  7. (optional) slap it inside of a sandwich, for better texture and some extra flavor.
